Mid-career professionals (ages 35-50) working in industries being disrupted by AI — copywriting, translation, basic legal research, financial analysis — need practical ways to evolve their careers without quitting their jobs or returning to full-time education. They have 5-10 hours per week, existing domain expertise that's becoming commoditized, and household financial obligations that prevent risk-taking. The problem is urgent: within 2-3 years, their current roles may shrink 40-60%. Existing reskilling platforms (Coursera, bootcamps, LinkedIn Learning) have <15% completion rates for this demographic because they ignore time pressure, identity crisis, and the need to earn while transitioning.
